Welcome to the colour contrast playground

This is a collection of tools and examples intented to fascilitate the promotion, experimentation and learing about high colour contrast on the web.

  • Injector: View your page with suggestec colour corrections. Colour combinations that do not meet WCAG are corrected. The links takes you to a sample page. You can apply this to your own page by including the javascript library.
  • Tweaker: Look up the contrast level of text-background colour pairs. This enhanced contrast calculator will suggest changes that ensure your colours meet WCAG AA and AAA success criteria. Colours are input using any valid CSS colour description (rgb, hsl, names, etc.).
  • Searcher: Interactively experiment and learn about how modifications of a colour affect the other colour while ensuring sufficient contrast.
  • Audit: Get a basic overview of colour usage on your webpage in terms of real-estate usage. The links take you to a sample page. You can audit your own page by including the javascrript libraries (see the source).
  • Extended audit: Get a more elaborate overview of colour usage on your webpage in terms of real-estate usage, colour harmonies and colour corrections. The links take you to a sample page. You can audit your own page by including the javascrript libraries (see the source). Note that the suggestions adhere to a contrast ratio of 4.5. Broswer-supported colour names are used as mnemonics.

The source code is available in the INJECTOR githup repository.
